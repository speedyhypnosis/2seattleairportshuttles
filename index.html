About Us

2SeattleAirportShuttles provides convenient, affordable airport transportation and town car service in Greater Seattle.

Founded by Seattle native William Anderson, we aim to offer travelers a reliable, stress-free ride to the airport with exemplary timeliness, safety, and hospitality.

Our team of professional local drivers leverages extensive knowledge of Seattle's terrain, traffic patterns, and airport to transport riders smoothly while avoiding unnecessary delays. We focus on 5-star service in clean, comfortable vehicles.

At 2SeattleAirportShuttles, we consider our customers like family. Don't hesitate to contact us to learn more about our airport shuttle and car services assisting travelers and locals.

FAQ

What areas do you provide airport shuttle service to?

We provide shuttle rides to and from Sea-Tac airport from Seattle downtown, Capitol Hill, Queen Anne, Ballard, West Seattle, Bellevue, and select hotels around the airport.

How do I make a reservation?

You can easily book online, use our app, or call our 24/7 dispatch office at 202-796-1499. We take reservations up to 7 days in advance.

Is there room for luggage and extra bags?

Yes, our vehicles have ample trunk space for luggage and equipment. Please notify us in advance for large amounts of group baggage.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, Apple Pay, Venmo, and payment apps. Cash and gratuities can be paid directly to drivers.

Why choose your shuttle service?

Our experienced local drivers, comfortable new vehicles, and exceptional hospitality are reflected in our 5-star passenger reviews. We are committed to reliable, quality transportation.

What happens if my flight is delayed?

Notify our team of any flight or schedule changes. We continuously track inbound flights and accommodate delays when able or reschedule rides as needed.
